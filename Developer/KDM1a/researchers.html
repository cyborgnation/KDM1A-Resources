Research Resources

LSD1/KDM1A mutations associated to a newly described form of intellectual disability impair demethylase activity and binding to transcription factors

Published in Human Molecular Genetics, April 2016. Analyzes the effects of KDM1A mutations in three patients on the structure, stability and activity of LSD1 using in vitro and cellular approaches. Concludes mutations lead to partial impairment of catalytic activity and perturb LSD1's ability to engage transcription factors that influence development.

Gene discovery for Mendelian conditions via social networking: de novo variants in KDM1A cause developmental delay and distinctive facial features

Published in Genetics in Medicine, December 2015. At that time, three known KDM1A patients existed. Notes that social networking is a powerful strategy to discover genes for rare Mendelian conditions. Authors were the developers of MyGene2.

De novo ANKRD11 and KDM1A gene mutations in a male with features of KBG syndrome and Kabuki syndrome

Published in American Journal of Medical Genetics, May 2014. First KDM1A case in medical literature. Posited KDM1A as source of developmental delays.
